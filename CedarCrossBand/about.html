About Cedar Cross Band

Welcome to the Cedar Cross Band practice song library. This collection of recordings is designed to help our band members practice and learn songs on their own time. The Cedar Cross Praise Band plays at the early (8:45 am) worship service at Cedar Cross United Methodist Church in Mill Creek WA.

About This Library

This practice library contains recordings of songs we perform and are learning. Each recording is available for streaming directly from this website, making it easy to practice anywhere you have internet access.

How to Use This Library

  • Browse: View all available songs in alphabetical order, with options to sort by title, date added, or filename.
  • Search: Find specific songs by title or keywords.
  • Play: Click any song title to stream the audio directly in your browser.
  • Practice: Listen repeatedly to learn your parts and improve your performance.

For Band Members

Band members can access this library anytime to:

  • Learn new songs before rehearsals
  • Review songs we've practiced
  • Refresh your memory on parts and arrangements
  • Practice at your own pace and schedule

Note: This library is regularly updated with new recordings. Check back often to find newly added practice songs.

Technical Information

Audio Format: All songs are in MP3 format for broad compatibility.
Browser Compatibility: This site works with all modern web browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
Mobile Friendly: You can access and stream songs from your phone or tablet.

Tips for Best Experience

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection for smooth streaming
  • Bookmark frequently used songs for quick access
  • Most browsers remember your volume and playback settings

Contact & Support

If you have questions about the practice library, encounter technical issues, or have suggestions for improvements, please contact the band administrator.

Questions about specific songs or arrangements?
Reach out to your fellow band members or the band leader.
